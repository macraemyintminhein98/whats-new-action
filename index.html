🚀 What's New Action

Automate your project's changelog! The What's New GitHub Action effortlessly generates a dynamic 'What's New' section for your README.md based on your latest merged pull requests.

Keep your users informed and your documentation up-to-date with zero manual effort.

  • Automated Updates: Never forget a changelog entry again.
  • Customizable Output: Control the section title and history depth.
  • Seamless Integration: Works directly within your existing GitHub workflow.
